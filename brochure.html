Our Story

India ran on sattu for 2,000 years. Then it forgot.

Sattu — roasted gram flour — has been the cornerstone of Indian nutrition since ancient times. Soldiers ate it. Farmers drank it. Wrestlers swore by it. It was India's original protein shake, long before protein shakes existed.

Then came packaged beverages. Flashy labels, aggressive marketing, and 30 grams of refined sugar per bottle. India traded its ancestral superfood for liquid sugar — and paid the price. Today, India has 101 million diabetics and rising.

RISE is bringing sattu back. Same 2,000-year recipe. Modern packaging. Ready-to-mix sachets that deliver 22g plant protein with zero refined sugar, zero chemicals, and zero compromise. This is the grain that made India rise — and it still will.
