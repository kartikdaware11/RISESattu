The strategic balance

Quick commerce wins on volume, D2C wins on margin. RISE scales through quick-commerce discovery, then converts repeat buyers to WhatsApp D2C — maximising LTV while keeping CAC low through referral loops.

D2C (Website + WhatsApp) + Zepto/Blinkit in Bengaluru. Validate unit economics and build the referral flywheel.

Modern Trade + Export

Tier-2 cities via kirana network. Modern trade (Reliance, DMart). UAE/UK NRI diaspora export channel.
