The Problem

India's health-conscious consumers have no good organic traditional drink option.

Pain #1 · Synthetic Drinks

Energy drinks & protein shakes loaded with sugar, artificial colors, preservatives. 33g of sugar in a single cola bottle — 7 teaspoons of harm.

Pain #2 · No Organic Option

Zero certified-organic traditional Indian health drink in the premium segment. A gap no national brand has filled.

Pain #3 · Overpriced Imports

Cold-pressed juices ₹100–150/serve. Protein shakes ₹80–150. No affordable desi option for the health-aware urban consumer.

Pain #4 · Lost Heritage

Our Solution

Sattu: India's original protein drink — reimagined for today.

Sattu — roasted gram flour — has been India's nutritional backbone for 2,000 years. 8–10g protein, 4g fiber, zero refined sugar per 30g serving. We've taken this ancient superfood and made it sachet-convenient, multi-flavored, and certified organic.
